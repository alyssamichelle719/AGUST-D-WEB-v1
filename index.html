About

Agust D is an alternate stage name for SUGA of BTS - real name Min Yoongi. He was born on March 9, 1993 in Daegu, South Korea. The stage name comes from two different factors: DT for his birth place Daegu Town, and SUGA spelled backwards as an ode to BTS. He started rapping and working on music at age 13, working part-time at age 17 before he ventured to Seoul to debut with BTS.

His first work under the name, a self titled mixtape released for free, was a more mature sound hitting topics such as depression and social anxiety. He speaks of real issues in his personal life and life itself, with a completely different sound than one he makes with BTS.

Among his solo projects, Min Yoongi is also an accomplished producer, making tracks for top artists in the industry such as PSY, IU, and remixed a Coldplay track. He has also composed ringtones for Samsung and the original title track for BTS In The Soop.

He writes, composes, arranges, and mixes all of his own work, with over 100 credits accounted for to date.
